    Licensing Compliance Analysis

    Educational Use Qualification

    CTRC BattleBots Curriculum qualifies for educational use because:

    1. Non-profit educational purpose
    2. CTRC is registered non-profit robotics club
    3. Curriculum freely available online
    4. No fees charged to students

    5. No revenue generated

    6. Proper attribution

    7. All sources cited with URLs
    8. Attribution maintained in curriculum

    9. Credit given to all creators

    10. Transformative use

    11. Images integrated into broader teaching context
    12. Used to demonstrate principles, not replace originals

    13. Serves different purpose than source material

    14. Limited excerpts

    15. Small number of images per source
    16. Does not reproduce entire original works

    17. Minimal necessary for educational goals

    18. No market harm

    19. Non-commercial use
    20. Does not compete with original sources
    21. Drives traffic to original sources via attribution

    Legal Basis

    Fair Use Doctrine (17 U.S.C. § 107): - ✅ Educational purpose - ✅ Factual/informational nature - ✅ Small portions used - ✅ No market harm - ✅ Transformative context

    Platform Terms Compliance: - ✅ Instructables: Educational use permitted - ✅ Thingiverse: Creative Commons licenses - ✅ Servo Magazine: Fair use of published content

    No formal permissions required, but courtesy notifications optional.
