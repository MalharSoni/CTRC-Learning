Three-Phase Learning Model

Learn, Build, and Prove your skills at every step

📚

Learn

Structured lessons teaching mechanics, programming, and CAD. Theory meets practice with clear explanations and examples.

🔧

Build

Hands-on projects where you apply what you learned. Build real mechanisms and complete robots using VEX parts.

🏆

Prove

Test your skills in challenges and competitions. Get immediate feedback and earn recognition for mastery.

V5 Foundation Program

Your roadmap from beginner to competition-ready

1
Unit 1

Drivetrain Mastery

Learn safety, VEX parts, structure, bracing, gear ratios, and friction. Build a competition-grade drivetrain from scratch.

2
Unit 2

Programming (LemLib)

Master VSCode, LemLib, drive controls, PID tuning, and autonomous routing. Program your robot to drive itself.

3
Unit 3

Onshape CAD

Design robots in 3D using Onshape. Learn sketching, modeling, assemblies, and rendering. Design before you build.

4
Unit 4

Mechanisms

Build wedges, intakes, and linear sliders. Learn how mechanisms work and when to use each design pattern.

5
Unit 5

Competition Ready

Integrate everything into a full robot. Learn match strategy, autonomous strategy, and compete in real 2v2 matches.
