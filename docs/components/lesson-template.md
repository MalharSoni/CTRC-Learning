  - `badge-hands-on` with `<i class="ph ph-wrench"></i>` — Hands-on
  - `badge-theory` with `<i class="ph ph-book-open"></i>` — Theory

**Timeline States:**

- `completed` — Green checkmark, slightly faded
- `current` — Blue, pulsing glow
- `upcoming` — Gray, hollow dot
