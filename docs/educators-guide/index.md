# Educator's Guide

Welcome to the CTRC Learning Educator's Guide. This section is designed for **mentors, teachers, and team leads** who want to use the CTRC Learning course with their students or team members.

## Who Is This For?

- VEX team mentors introducing CAD to new members
- Teachers running robotics programs in schools
- Team leads onboarding students into design workflows

## How to Use This Guide

This guide will help you:

- **Plan a curriculum** around the CTRC Learning Course stages
- **Set pacing expectations** for students at different skill levels
- **Assess progress** through exercises and design reviews
- **Supplement learning** with hands-on build activities

## Getting Started

!!! tip "Start with Stage 1"
    We recommend all new students begin with [Unit 1](../foundation/unit1/index.md) of the V5 Foundation Program, regardless of prior CAD experience. The fundamentals covered there establish a shared vocabulary and workflow.

## Suggested Pacing

| Stage | Recommended Time | Notes |
|-------|-----------------|-------|

| Stage 1 | 4-6 sessions | Core fundamentals — don't rush this |
