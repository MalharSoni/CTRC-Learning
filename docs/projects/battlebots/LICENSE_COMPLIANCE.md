## Educational Use Statement

### CTRC's Use of Third-Party Content

The CTRC BattleBots Curriculum is a **non-profit educational project** that:

1. **Serves educational purposes exclusively**
   - Teaching high school students combat robotics
   - Developing STEM skills (CAD, physics, engineering design)
   - Preparing students for NHRL competitions

2. **Is non-commercial**
   - CTRC is a registered non-profit robotics club
   - Curriculum is freely available online
   - No fees charged to students
   - No revenue generated from curriculum

3. **Provides proper attribution**
   - All third-party content is properly cited
   - Source URLs provided for verification
   - Attribution maintained in curriculum materials

4. **Uses content transformatively**
   - Images integrated into broader teaching context
   - Combined with original CTRC instructional content
   - Serves different purpose than original (teaching vs. build documentation)

5. **Uses limited excerpts**
   - Small number of images from each source
   - Does not reproduce entire original works
   - Minimal necessary content to achieve educational goals

---

### Legal Basis for Third-Party Content Use

#### Fair Use Doctrine (17 U.S.C. § 107)

Educational fair use permits limited use of copyrighted material without permission for:
- Teaching in non-profit educational institutions
- Scholarship and research
- Criticism, comment, and news reporting

**Our use qualifies because:**
- ✅ Non-profit educational purpose
- ✅ Factual/informational nature of source material
- ✅ Small portions used relative to whole works
- ✅ No market harm to original creators
- ✅ Transformative educational context

## Dispute Resolution

If any content creator requests removal or modification:

1. **Immediate Response:** Within 48 hours of notification
2. **Good Faith Discussion:** Work with creator to resolve concerns
3. **Removal if Requested:** Content will be removed within 7 days if creator insists
4. **Alternative Sources:** CTRC will source alternative images/content
5. **Documentation:** All requests and resolutions documented

**Contact for Licensing Questions:**
