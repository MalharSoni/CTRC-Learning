## Print Orientation Diagrams (1)

### ✅ hole-orientation-comparison.jpg
**Location:** `/docs/projects/battlebots/images/printing/`
**Source:** Servo Magazine - "Maximizing Strength for Printing Combat Robots with a 3D Printer"
**Size:** 70KB
**Description:** Side-by-side comparison showing horizontal vs vertical hole orientation in 3D printed parts. Demonstrates why vertically-oriented holes are structurally superior for stress-bearing features.
**Use in:** Module 6 - Layer orientation principles section
**Quality:** Excellent - clear diagram with labels

**Key Teaching Points:**
- Horizontally-printed holes = weak points at layer lines
- Vertically-printed holes = solid plastic surrounding hole
- Critical for weapon axles, motor mounts, load-bearing features
