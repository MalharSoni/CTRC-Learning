## Primary References

### Combat Robot Design Handbooks

**Repeat Robotics Beetleweight Combat Robot Design Handbook**
- **URL:** https://repeat-robotics.com/handbook/
- **Author:** Peter Garnache (Repeat Robotics)
- **Content:** Comprehensive design guide covering weight budgets, weapon physics, drivetrain calculations, and material selection for beetleweight robots
- **How We Use It:** Referenced for weapon energy calculations, bite formulas, and general design principles

**Duke Combat Robotics Wiki**
- **URL:** https://wiki.duke-combat-robotics.org/
- **Maintained by:** Duke University Combat Robotics Team
- **Content:** Technical articles on drive systems, weapon design, electronics, and safety protocols
- **How We Use It:** Referenced for technical specifications and safety best practices

**Just 'Cuz Robotics Combat Robot Resources**
- **URL:** https://www.justcuzrobotics.com/
- **Content:** Educational resources for antweight and beetleweight builders
- **How We Use It:** Design principles and component selection guidance
