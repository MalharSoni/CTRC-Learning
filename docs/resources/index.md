# Resources

A curated collection of external resources for VEX V5 robot design and Onshape CAD. These links supplement the learning course with additional tutorials, community knowledge, and vendor information.

---

## Onshape Learning

### Official Onshape Resources
- **[Onshape Learning Center](https://learn.onshape.com/)** — Free structured courses directly from Onshape covering all skill levels
- **[Onshape Help Documentation](https://cad.onshape.com/help)** — Complete reference for every feature and tool in Onshape
- **[Onshape YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@Onshape)** — Video tutorials and webinars
- **[Onshape Forum](https://forum.onshape.com/)** — Community Q&A for Onshape-specific questions

### VEX-Specific Onshape Tutorials
- **[VEX V5 CAD Resources](https://www.vexrobotics.com/)** — Official VEX resources for robot design in Onshape
- **[frcdesign.org](https://www.frcdesign.org/)** — A well-known CAD learning resource that inspired this site (FRC-focused but many concepts transfer to VEX)

## VEX Community

### Discussion Forums
- **[VEX Forum](https://www.vexforum.com/)** — The largest VEX community forum. Design discussion, robot reveals, strategy, and more. Essential reading for any serious VEX designer.
- **[VEX Discord](https://discord.gg/vrc)** — Real-time chat with VEX community members. Has channels for CAD help, design discussion, and team collaboration.
- **[r/vex on Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/vex/)** — VEX community on Reddit

### Technical Resources
- **[RobotEvents](https://www.robotevents.com/)** — Match data, event results, and match videos for every VEX event
- **[VEX Via](https://via.vex.com/)** — Skills rankings and team statistics
- **[VEX V5 Game Manual](https://www.vexrobotics.com/v5/competition)** — Official rules (updated annually)

### Open-Source Robot Designs
Many top teams publish their robot CAD after the season. Study these to learn from the best:

- Search VEX Forum for "robot reveal" or "CAD release" threads
- Look for teams who share their entire design process publicly
- Check team websites and GitHub pages for Onshape document links

!!! tip "Study Top Teams"
    Many top VEX teams are known for exceptional design and public knowledge sharing. Search for robot reveals and CAD releases on the VEX Forum to learn from the best.

## Calculators and Tools

### Design Calculators
- **[ReCalc](https://www.reca.lc/)** — Robotics calculator for arms, elevators, drivetrains, flywheels, and pneumatics
- **[JVN Design Calculator](https://johnvneun.com/calc)** — Classic motor and mechanism calculator (useful for general robotics design)
- **[Belt Calculator (WCP)](https://wcproducts.com/pages/belt-length-calculator)** — Calculate belt lengths for HTD belt systems

### CAD Utilities
- **[MKCAD](https://appstore.onshape.com/)** — Robotics part library for Onshape (search in Onshape App Store)
- **FeatureScripts** — Custom features in Onshape (Tube Converter, Shaft Generator, Belt & Chain, Gusset, Lighten)
